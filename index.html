More about me

Hello, I’m Lixxie. My journey began with a fascination for game development, which led me to Blender. Driven by a surge of creative ideas, I immersed myself in learning 3D asset creation. With Blender under my belt, I dove into Unreal Engine, eager to bring my game concepts to life. However, despite my enthusiasm, I struggled to complete projects, often falling victim to laziness and burnout. I took a hiatus from game development but continued to explore Blender's possibilities. Eventually, my curiosity steered me toward Python, igniting a new passion for creating interactive tools. My first Python project was a Discord bot named LucidGhost, but hosting it on Replit proved too costly due to their shifting pricing. Undeterred, I turned my attention to personal projects designed to simplify my life. Recognizing the potential to help others, I launched a GitHub repository called Jtools, offering a collection of free and open-source tools. Though there’s still much for me to explore, I’m committed to moving forward, one step at a time.
